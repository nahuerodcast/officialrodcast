Rodcast is a music producer and DJ from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

He started at the early age of 12 years old producing tracks in his bedroom and making music for the internet with colleagues. He had several projects before Rodcast including Sunrise with more than 4 million streams on Spotify, Soundcloud and other platforms.

With several supports from Lexlay, Happy Techno owner, his release "Boogie With You" scaled quickly to the charts on Beatport, landing the #19 ranking on the Top 100 Tech House Releases.
